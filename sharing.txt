Sharing Guide
1.) Naming Convention
Controller
    - Resource controllers (with CRUD) should be plural.

2.) Definition
Services
    - Refers to business logic that may not be involved in manipulating database.
    - Connecting to third party application.

Traits
    - Re-useable functions
    - Should not be used to replace the function of a service or helper.

Helpers
    - Functions that are not business logic and does not involve on connecting to db server.
    - Mainly used to manipulate data.
        - formatting date
        - transformming data
    - light functions only

Repositories
    - Where we write our queries.
    - Repositories will be controlled by interfaces.

-------

Frontend can use resource from admin, but changing is not allowed. (If want to change, create another separate resource for the frontend.)
